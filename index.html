Selected work

Three technical threads.

01

Numerical quantum dynamics and detection-time simulations

Python/CuPy finite-difference workflows for Schrödinger/Pauli detector models with absorbing boundaries, complex absorbing potentials, sparse non-Hermitian systems, Crank–Nicolson / GMRES workflows, probability-current diagnostics, norm-loss checks, trajectory sampling, and publication-oriented post-processing.

Research-code evidence · numerical simulation · detector-model physics

02

Spin–momentum filtering at a spin-coupled absorbing boundary

Numerical/theoretical work on Pauli dynamics in a harmonic waveguide, spinor absorbing-boundary simulations, boundary-symbol diagnostics, finite-window detection statistics, selected data, figures, and animations.

Preprint / manuscript · boundary mechanisms · reproducibility material

YBCO Josephson diode / Josephson ratchet analysis

Superconducting-device R&D material covering cryogenic I–V analysis, critical-current asymmetry, rectification, loaded operation, output power, thermodynamic efficiency, and measurement-chain context for YBCO Josephson devices.
