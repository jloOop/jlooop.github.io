Selected work

Three technical threads.

01

Numerical quantum dynamics and detection-time simulations

Python/CuPy finite-difference workflows for Schrödinger/Pauli detector models with absorbing boundaries, complex absorbing potentials, probability-current diagnostics, norm-loss checks, trajectory sampling, and publication-oriented post-processing.

Repository arXiv
02

Spin–momentum filtering at an absorbing boundary

Numerical/theoretical work on spinor absorbing-boundary simulations, Pauli dynamics in a harmonic waveguide, boundary-symbol diagnostics, finite-window detection statistics, selected data, figures, and animations.

Repository arXiv
03

YBCO Josephson diode / Josephson ratchet analysis

Superconducting-device R&D material covering cryogenic I–V analysis, critical-current asymmetry, rectification, loaded operation, output power, efficiency, and measurement-chain context for YBCO Josephson devices.
